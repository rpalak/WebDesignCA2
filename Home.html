WHAT'S THE OCCASION

Whatever your occasion, let us at Cherry Cakes make it extra special with a custom designed celebration cake.Cherry Cakes offers complete cake design and decoration service. You can choose from a number of different cake flavours and designs and we will work with you to design a cake that is fitting for your specific event. Tell us about the individual for whom it is intended, their hobbies, the theme of the occasion and the statement you are trying to create. Cherry can offer classes for you learn cake decorating skills to use at home. We offer cupcake, macaron and basic cake decorating classes. Our classes are held in our purpose build cake kitchen situated in Lake Estate.

CUPCAKES PARTIES

We also offer cupcake parties for children’s birthdays (although we can do cupcake parties for all ages!). These range from a basic party where children are able to decorate their cupcakes and takeaway boxes, to parties where we are able to demonstrate how to make cupcakes from scratch. If you have a specific cupcake design or themed party we can work with that too - for example if you have a ladybird themed party, we could make lady bird cupcakes!

WE CREATE

We create wedding cakes, birthday cakes, graduation cakes, retirement cakes,anniversary cakes, baby shower cakes, christening cakes, Christmas cakes or any other special cake!

CUPCAKES

As well as cake design we offer cupcakes in various flavours and designs. Cupcakes make a great alternative to celebration cakes and can be displayed on cupcakes stands as a replacement to a normal cake. Minimum order of 12 cupcakes. Alternatively, during summer these can be purchased individually at the Queenstown Farmers Market on Church Street on a Saturday morning between 9am - 12pm from December to March

MACAROONS

The latest product offering from Cherry Blossom Cakes are the stunning and oh so cute French Macaroon. These come and a large range of colours and flavours (Vanilla,Chocolate,Lemon, Strawberry, Raspberry and White Chocolate, Hazelnut, Coconut and Pineapple, Mango, Cherry Ripe, Pistachio, Passionfruit, Mint and Dark Chocolate to name a few...). Macaroons can be ordered in boxes of 24 or bought individually at the Remarkables and Queenstown Farmers Market.
