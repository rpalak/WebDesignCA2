About Cherry Cakes

Cherry Cakes is based in Queenstown serving Dublin-1, Dublin-2, Dublin-3,Dublin-5,Dublin-7 and the surrounding areas. Cherry Cakes specialises in cakes and cupcakes for all types of events. Cherry Cakes aim to create beautiful cakes that perfectly compliment your event.

Melissa grew up in Hawkes Bay and studied at National College Of Ireland in Dublin-1 before embarking on a career in Human Resources. After moving to London she specialised in Graduate Recruitment for the Investment Banking industry for 5 years. After returning to Ireland and spending time with her parents (who now live just outside of Arrowtown), Melissa decided that the Queenstown area was just too beautiful to live anywhere else.

For years Melissa has delighted friends, family and colleges with home made treats and now is making it into more than just a hobby. Melissa started baking with her mother when she was young and has come along way from the bright pink marshmallow slice they use to make every Sunday (although she still loves things that are pink and sweet, so some things never change). More recently Melissa has studied cake decoration through Planet Cake, one of Ireland’s leading cake designers.
